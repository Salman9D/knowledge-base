## Core Vision

Founded by AI pioneer **Fei-Fei Li**, World Labs is focused on the next frontier of AI: **Spatial Intelligence**. While LLMs understand text and VLMs understand pixels, World Labs is building models that understand the **underlying 3D physics and state transitions** of the world.

- **Scale**: Raised **$1 billion** in 2026 to accelerate model training.
- **Goal**: Move from "viewing" images to "reasoning" about physical volumes, object permanency, and physical interactions.

## Strategic Importance

World Labs represents the "Intelligence Layer" that 9D Technologies aspires to integrate. Their models could provide the foundational "brain" for a spatial assistant to understand that a cup is still on a table even when the camera is turned away, or to predict how a physical environment might change.
