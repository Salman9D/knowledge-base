# Brand Activation Visual Compliance

**Summary**: Phone-based tool for brand managers to verify that retail displays (POSMs) are correctly installed in shops.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
FMCG brands (Coca-Cola, Nestle) spend millions on "Retail Displays" (POSMs) but have zero visibility on whether they are actually installed or hidden in the back of the shop. A merchandiser snaps a photo; the vision model confirms it is the correct display in the correct location.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to identify specific brand activation displays (POSMs) and verify their correct placement and assembly within a retail environment.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides phone-based visual guidance for merchandisers, highlighting the ideal display location and overlaying assembly instructions or compliance scores directly on the screen.

## Grounding
"Display leakage" is a 20-30% loss in marketing spend for Pakistani FMCGs.

## MOAT
Visual dataset of thousands of Pakistani Kiryana and General Store environments.

## Scoring Debate
- **Solution Quality (8)**: Direct audit tool for brand managers.
- **Adoption Criteria (9)**: Extremely high; brands already employ "merchandisers" who can use this app.
- **Moat Confidence (7)**: Moderate; SKU/Display recognition is a known tech, but the local shop context is the data barrier.
