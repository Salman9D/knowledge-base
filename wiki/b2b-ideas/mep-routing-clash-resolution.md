# Spatial MEP Routing & Clash Resolution for Hyperscale Data Centers

![[problem_23_img1.jpeg]]
![[problem_23_img2.jpeg]]

**Summary**: Real-time tracking against digital twins to resolve MEP routing mistakes and clashes in high-precision data center construction.

**Sources**: [[B2B Problem Statements (AR_VR_MR).pdf]]

---

## Description
Routing MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing) systems in hyperscale data centers is an exercise in extreme density and precision. A routing mistake of just 1cm can cascade into major clashes, delaying multi-million dollar construction schedules. Traditional paper plans and even handheld tablets are insufficient for verifying as-built status against the design in real-time.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Continuously compares the live installation progress against the high-fidelity Digital Twin (BIM model). It uses computer vision to detect installation variances and potential future clashes before they happen.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides workers with a holographic guide of the intended routing paths. Clashes are highlighted in red directly in the worker's field of view, allowing for immediate correction.

## Grounding
As data center investments grow, the speed and accuracy of MEP installation become critical competitive factors. Reducing rework and schedule delays provides a massive ROI for general contractors and specialized MEP firms.

## MOAT
Confidential design topologies of hyperscale facilities and a proprietary database of installation variance data that allows the AI to predict which types of routings are most prone to human error.
