# Seed Purity & Germination Vision Lab

**Summary**: Tablet-based visual analysis of seed samples to identify weed seeds or inert matter before sale.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Local seed companies sell "adulterated" seeds mixed with weed seeds. A technician spreads a seed sample on a tablet-monitored tray; the vision model identifies and counts non-target seeds in seconds.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to analyze seed samples, automatically identifying and counting weed seeds, inert matter, or non-target varieties in seconds.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual guidance, highlighting every detected impurity or non-compliant seed on the tray and displaying an immediate purity percentage on-screen.

## Grounding
Farmers in Punjab suffer massive losses due to poor seed quality. "Fake seeds" are a major political and economic issue.

## MOAT
A visual catalog of local weed seeds and Pakistani seed varieties.

## Scoring Debate
- **Solution Quality (8)**: Automates a tedious manual task; prevents "bad batches" from hitting the market.
- **Adoption Criteria (7)**: Medium; target is the 1,000+ local seed companies, many of which are small-scale.
- **Moat Confidence (9)**: Very high; unique weed profiles in Pakistan.
