# Construction BIM Deviation Tracker

**Summary**: Tablet-based comparison of physical construction to BIM models to catch errors early.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Tablet compares printed plans / BIM models to the actual concrete poured, flagging errors before they set.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to compare the physical concrete structure with the digital BIM model, detecting deviations in dimensions or placement with millimeter precision.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides a tablet-first overlay that projects the digital BIM model onto the live construction site, visually highlighting exactly where the physical build deviates from the plan.

## Grounding
Rework is the biggest cost in Bahria Town, DHA, and CPEC infrastructure builds.

"What goes wrong in Pakistani construction" dataset.
