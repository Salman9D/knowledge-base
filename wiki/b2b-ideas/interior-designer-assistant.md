# AI Spatial Interior Designer Assistant

![[problem_29_img1.jpeg]]
![[problem_29_img2.jpeg]]

**Summary**: Room scanning and real-time furniture visualization within specific budget and style constraints for interior design.

**Sources**: [[B2B Problem Statements (AR_VR_MR).pdf]]

---

## Description
Interior design is often a process of trial and error, which can be expensive and time-consuming. Clients struggle to visualize how furniture will look in their space, and designers struggle to stay within strict budget limitations. 
A spatial AI assistant can scan a room in seconds and provide a fully furnished AR visualization that respects both the physical dimensions and the financial constraints of the project.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Automatically measures room dimensions, detects existing light sources, and suggests furniture layouts. It uses "design preference intelligence" to match items with the user's style while filtering for budget compatibility.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Allows users to visualize furniture pieces, wall colors, and floorings in real-time within their actual space using a smartphone or AR headset.

## Grounding
The home renovation and interior design market is growing as urban populations increase. Providing a low-friction way for consumers and professional designers to visualize and purchase furniture can significantly increase sales conversion for retailers.

## MOAT
Proprietary interior spatial datasets (billions of room configurations) and a design preference engine that learns the aesthetic nuances of different regional and demographic segments.
