# Surgical Instrument QC & Compliance

**Summary**: Vision assistant to verify exact geometries, polishes, and stamping of surgical instruments before export.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]], [[spatial-ai-sync-2026-05-07]]

---

## Description
Sialkot produces 70% of the world's surgical instruments. A vision assistant verifies exact geometries, polishes, and stamping before export packaging.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to verify the exact geometry, polish quality, and stamping of surgical instruments against strict medical compliance standards.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual guidance, highlighting microscopic defects or geometric deviations on the instrument's surface and guiding the inspector through the final QC checklist.

## Grounding
Medical export requires extreme FDA/CE compliance. Hand-eye QC is currently slow and error-prone.

## MOAT
Highly specific dataset of surgical tool tolerances and manufacturing micro-defects.
