# Remote Surgical Mentorship

**Summary**: AR-guided incisions for junior doctors in rural areas, mentored by surgeons in major cities.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
A surgeon in Karachi uses AR to guide a junior doctor operating in rural Sindh, overlaying incisions on the live video feed.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to track surgical instruments and anatomical landmarks, providing spatial context for both the on-site and remote surgeon.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides AR-guided visual cues, allowing a remote mentor to project precise incision lines and guidance markers directly into the junior doctor's field of view in real-time.

## Grounding
Massive healthcare disparity between tier-1 cities and rural areas in Pakistan.

## MOAT
Annotated surgical video datasets specific to local pathology.
