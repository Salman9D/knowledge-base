# Fiber-Optic Splice Quality Vision

**Summary**: Tablet-based visual verification of fiber-optic splicing for telecom contractors.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Telecom contractors (Nayatel, PTCL) often suffer from "bad splices" that cause signal loss. A technician snaps a photo of the completed splice; the vision model confirms the alignment and cleanliness before the cabinet is closed.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to analyze the alignment and cleanliness of fiber-optic splices, detecting microscopic flaws that cause signal loss.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual guidance, highlighting the precise point of misalignment or contamination on the screen and confirming "splice success" before the technician closes the cabinet.

## Grounding
Redoing a splice in a remote area is a massive operational cost. First-time-right is critical for fiber rollout.

## MOAT
Visual dataset of "failing" splices across different fiber brands used in Pakistan.

## Scoring Debate
- **Solution Quality (8)**: Prevents repeat truck rolls.
- **Adoption Criteria (8)**: Contractors are penalized for signal loss; they have a high incentive to use this.
- **Moat Confidence (8)**: Strong; specific to the hardware used by Pakistani telcos.
