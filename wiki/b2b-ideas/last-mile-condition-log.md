# Last-Mile Delivery Proof-of-Condition (Daraz/TCS)

**Summary**: Automated mobile scanning of parcels during handover to identify tampering or damage before the customer accepts.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
A smartphone-based vision tool for delivery riders. It scans the parcel's exterior at the doorstep, automatically identifying broken seals, crushed corners, or moisture damage. It generates a "Trust Score" for the delivery.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to automatically detect tampered seals, crushed corners, or moisture damage on parcels during the delivery handover.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides phone-based visual guidance for riders, highlighting detected damage on the parcel's surface and guiding them through a quick logging process to generate a "Trust Score."

## Grounding
E-commerce "returns" in Pakistan are plagued by disputes over who damaged the item (shipper, rider, or customer).

## MOAT
Dataset of parcel damage patterns in the harsh Pakistani logistics environment (dust, heat, rain, bike-handling).

## Scoring Debate
- **Solution Quality (7)**: Good for dispute resolution, though riders might find it time-consuming.
- **Adoption Criteria (9)**: Uses existing rider smartphones. High willingness from logistics giants (TCS, Daraz) to reduce liability.
- **Moat Confidence (7)**: Moderate; Big Tech could build this, but lack the local logistics context.
