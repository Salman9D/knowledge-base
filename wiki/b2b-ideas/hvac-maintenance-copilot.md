# Commercial HVAC Maintenance Copilot

**Summary**: Visual overlay for technicians to maintain large HVAC units in commercial buildings.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Visual overlay for technicians maintaining massive HVAC units in malls (Packages, Dolmen) and corporate towers. Identifies valves, filters, and logs maintenance.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to identify valves, filters, and electrical components within massive HVAC units, tracking their maintenance status.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides hands-free (or tablet-first) visual guidance, overlaying maintenance checklists and repair instructions directly onto the identified components in the technician's view.

## Grounding
HVAC power consumption is the largest operational cost for local commercial real estate.

## MOAT
Asset lifecycle data for major commercial real estate environments.
