# Electric Fan Motor Winding Verification

**Summary**: Vision assistant to ensure the correct number of copper turns and insulation placement in ceiling fan motors.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Gujrat is the hub for fan manufacturing. "Winding" is often done by semi-automated machines but has high error rates. A tablet scans the completed winding, counting turns and ensuring no wire is touching the metal core (short circuit risk).

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to count copper turns and inspect insulation placement, identifying potential short-circuit risks or winding errors on-device.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual guidance, highlighting the specific area of a winding error and overlaying the correct winding pattern to assist workers in making immediate corrections.

## Grounding
Poor winding leads to "motor burnout," the #1 cause of fan returns in the local market.

## MOAT
Visual dataset of motor winding configurations for the most common Pakistani fan brands (GFC, Royal, Pak Fan).

## Scoring Debate
- **Solution Quality (8)**: Replaces unreliable manual spark testing.
- **Adoption Criteria (7)**: Gujrat manufacturers are looking to improve quality to export to Africa and the Middle East.
- **Moat Confidence (9)**: Specific to local fan motor geometries.
