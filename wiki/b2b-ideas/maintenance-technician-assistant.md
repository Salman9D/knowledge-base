# Spatial Maintenance Technician Assistant

![[problem_30_img2.jpeg]]

**Summary**: AR-guided repair and machine state awareness for industrial technicians, leveraging captured senior expertise and knowledge graphs.

**Sources**: [[B2B Problem Statements (AR_VR_MR).pdf]]

---

## Description
Industrial maintenance suffers from a significant "skills gap" as senior technicians retire and equipment becomes more complex. Junior technicians often lack the "tacit knowledge" required to diagnose and repair specialized machinery. 
A spatial assistant captures senior expertise into an industrial knowledge graph, providing junior technicians with real-time, AR-guided repair instructions.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Identifies the specific machine and its current state (e.g., thermal signatures, vibration patterns) using computer vision and sensor fusion. it retrieves relevant troubleshooting steps from a proprietary industrial knowledge graph.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Step-by-step repair procedures, safety warnings, and "virtual hands" from senior experts are overlaid directly on the physical machinery, guiding the technician through complex assemblies.

## Grounding
Reducing unplanned downtime is a primary goal for manufacturing plants. Empowering less-experienced technicians to perform high-quality repairs reduces the reliance on a shrinking pool of senior experts and lowers maintenance costs.

## MOAT
A deep industrial knowledge graph linking equipment states to specific failure outcomes and repair successes, enriched by thousands of hours of captured maintenance sessions.
