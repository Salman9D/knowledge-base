# Dairy/Livestock Health Monitoring

**Summary**: Vision scanning of cattle to detect lameness, weight loss, or skin diseases early.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Vision scanning of cattle at feeding stations (Engro Foods, FrieslandCampina). Detects lameness, weight loss, or skin diseases early.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to monitor cattle movement and body condition, automatically detecting signs of lameness, weight loss, or skin diseases on-device.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual feedback, highlighting specific animals that require medical attention and overlaying health metrics and vaccination history directly onto the live feed of the herd.

## Grounding
Corporate dairy farming is scaling rapidly; animal health directly impacts milk yield.

## MOAT
Visual phenotypic data mapped to milk production in the local climate.
