# Warehouse Loading Space Optimizer

**Summary**: Tablet-based scanning of a truck's cargo bed to suggest the most optimal way to stack mixed-size parcels.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Truck loading in Pakistan is often chaotic and inefficient. A loader points a tablet at the truck; the vision model measures the dimensions and suggests the placement of the "next parcel" based on size and weight constraints (e.g., heavy items on bottom).

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to measure truck cargo dimensions and parcel sizes, calculating the most efficient stacking configuration based on weight and volume.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual guidance, projecting the optimal placement for the "next parcel" directly into the truck bed, helping loaders maximize space utilization.

## Grounding
Fuel costs are rising. Improving truck "fill rates" by even 10% directly impacts the bottom line for logistics companies.

## MOAT
Dataset of "irregular parcel types" and truck dimensions common in Pakistan (e.g., Hino/Mazda trucks).

## Scoring Debate
- **Solution Quality (8)**: Significantly improves cubic utilization.
- **Adoption Criteria (8)**: High interest from mid-sized logistics firms looking to save fuel.
- **Moat Confidence (7)**: Moderate; standard geometry problem, but local "truck type" data is the edge.
