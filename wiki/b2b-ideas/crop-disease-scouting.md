# Crop Disease & Pest Scouting

**Summary**: On-device vision for identifies local pests and diseases in cotton and wheat crops.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Agronomists use tablets/phones in the field to scan cotton or wheat leaves. The on-device vision identifies specific local pests (e.g., Whitefly, Pink Bollworm).

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using on-device computer vision to identify local pests and diseases on cotton and wheat leaves with high accuracy.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual guidance, highlighting infected areas of the plant and overlaying recommended treatment steps or pesticide dosages directly in the field.

## Grounding
Agriculture is 22% of Pakistan's GDP. Delayed pest response ruins yields.
