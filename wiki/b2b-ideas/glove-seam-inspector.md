# Gloves Seam-Strength Vision Inspector

**Summary**: Vision assistant to check the stitch density and seam overlap on industrial safety gloves.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Sialkot is a major exporter of working/safety gloves. If a seam fails during a "stress test" by an international lab, the whole batch is rejected. A tablet scans the seams, ensuring the number of stitches per inch meets ISO standards.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to verify stitch density and seam overlap, ensuring every industrial safety glove meets ISO standards for seam strength.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual guidance, highlighting areas where the stitch count is low or the seam is misaligned, allowing for immediate quality correction.

## Grounding
Safety compliance is non-negotiable for European/US buyers. Manual counting of stitches is impossible at scale.

## MOAT
Dataset of seam patterns for heavy-duty leather and synthetic safety gloves.

## Scoring Debate
- **Solution Quality (8)**: Automates a critical compliance check.
- **Adoption Criteria (8)**: High; Sialkot glove makers are already highly compliant and looking for automation.
- **Moat Confidence (9)**: Highly specialized industrial data.
