# Spatial Digital Twins for Brownfield Industrial Plants

![[problem_12_img1.jpeg]]
![[problem_12_img2.jpeg]]

**Summary**: Auto-generating digital twins for brownfield refineries and chemical plants using LiDAR and photogrammetry to optimize maintenance and retrofitting.

**Sources**: [[B2B Problem Statements (AR_VR_MR).pdf]]

---

## Description
Brownfield industrial sites, such as older refineries and chemical plants, often lack accurate, up-to-date 3D CAD models. This makes planning retrofits, performing maintenance, and ensuring safety compliance extremely difficult. Manual surveying is slow and prone to error, while existing "as-built" documentation is frequently outdated or missing.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **Automated Twin Generation**: Uses LiDAR and photogrammetry to rapidly scan facilities and auto-generate high-fidelity 3D digital twins.
- **Feature Recognition**: AI models identify pipes, valves, and structural elements within the point cloud to create semantically rich models.
- **Spatial Documentation**: Maintenance logs and sensor data are anchored to specific physical components within the digital twin.

## Grounding
Pakistan's industrial base consists largely of aging brownfield facilities in the textile, chemical, and fertilizer sectors. As these industries seek to modernize and improve efficiency, the lack of accurate spatial data is a major bottleneck. Providing a cost-effective way to "digitize the physical" allows these plants to implement advanced Industry 4.0 practices without needing a total rebuild.

## MOAT
The moat is derived from the scarcity of training data for complex, aging industrial environments. By creating facility-specific twins that incorporate years of local maintenance logs and operational data, the platform creates a "platform-level + customer-level" moat. The more plants scanned, the better the AI becomes at recognizing idiosyncratic industrial components.

## Scoring Debate
- **Solution Quality (8)**: Provides a foundational digital layer for industries that are currently operating "blind" in 3D space.
- **Adoption Criteria (8)**: High demand for retrofitting and efficiency improvements in established industrial sectors.
- **Moat Confidence (8)**: Strong defensibility through proprietary datasets of industrial layouts and component recognition models.
