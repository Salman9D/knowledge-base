# Cement Kiln Refractory Inspection

**Summary**: Vision-based wear analysis for cement kilns during shutdowns.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Vision assistant analyzes thermal imaging and structural wear inside massive cement kilns during shutdown, guiding repair crews.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to analyze thermal and structural data, identifying micro-cracks or excessive refractory wear that might be missed by the human eye.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides hands-free visual guidance for repair crews, overlaying thermal maps and structural blueprints directly onto the kiln's interior walls to highlight precise areas needing repair.

## Grounding
Cement is a massive local industry; kiln downtime costs millions of Rupees daily.

## MOAT
Kiln wear-and-tear models specific to local raw material composition.
