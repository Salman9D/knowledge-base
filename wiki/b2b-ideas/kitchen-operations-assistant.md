# Large-Scale Kitchen Operations

**Summary**: Tablet/AR task overlays for chefs in commercial kitchens to manage timing and safety.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]], [[spatial-ai-sync-2026-05-07]]

---

## Description
Tablet/AR task overlays for chefs in massive catering or hospital kitchens. Automates recipe timing, hazard warnings, and hygiene compliance logging.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to track recipe timing, monitor hygiene compliance (e.g., hand washing), and detect potential safety hazards like spills.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides hands-free visual guidance for chefs, projecting task timers, ingredient measurements, and safety warnings directly onto their workspace or via a wall-mounted tablet.

## Grounding
From May 07 Sync: Fast-paced, high-stakes environment with massive waste potential.

## MOAT
Proprietary workflow and compliance data for high-volume local kitchens.
