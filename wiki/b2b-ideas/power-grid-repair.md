# Power Grid Substation Repair

**Summary**: AR/Tablet safety checklists and troubleshooting for high-voltage grid repairs.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
AR/Tablet safety checklist and step-by-step repair guide for high-voltage grid components.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to identify high-voltage components, detect signs of aging or damage, and verify that all safety protocols are being followed.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides hands-free (or tablet-first) visual guidance, overlaying detailed circuit diagrams and step-by-step repair checklists directly onto the physical grid components to prevent hazardous errors.

## Grounding
Grid failures are frequent; maintenance safety is notoriously poor.

## MOAT
Extremely niche data on local grid aging and failure cascades.
