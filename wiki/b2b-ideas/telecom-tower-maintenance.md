# Telecom Tower Remote Maintenance

**Summary**: Tablet-based component identification and troubleshooting for telecom tower technicians.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Field technician points tablet at tower panels. Assistant identifies components and overlays troubleshooting steps; NOC engineer can annotate live.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to identify tower components and detect equipment faults or cabling errors in complex telecom environments.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides hands-free (or tablet-first) visual guidance, overlaying troubleshooting steps and allowing remote engineers to project annotations and instructions directly onto the field technician's view.

## Grounding
High turnover of skilled techs; vast rural tower networks.

## MOAT
Telecom asset fault history per specific local carrier infrastructure.
