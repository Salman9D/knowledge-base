# Rice/Wheat Export Grading Station

**Summary**: Camera-based grading of grains for impurities and quality metrics before export.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Fixed tablet/camera rigs over conveyor belts grading Basmati rice or wheat for impurities, broken grains, or discoloration before export packing.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to scan grains on conveyor belts, identifying broken grains, discoloration, and foreign impurities with high precision.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first or station-fixed visual feedback, highlighting detected impurities in the live stream and displaying real-time quality metrics for each batch.

## Grounding
Food exports require strict grading; human eyes suffer fatigue.

## MOAT
Massive dataset of grain quality metrics tied to local suppliers.
