# Port Wharf-to-Truck Tally Assistant

**Summary**: Tablet-based automated counting and logging of bags (cement/sugar/grain) as they are loaded onto trucks from ships.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Manual tallying (counting bags) at Karachi port is prone to theft and error. A tablet pointed at the loading ramp automatically counts every bag, logs the timestamp, and snaps a photo of the truck license plate.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to automatically count bags being loaded onto trucks, while simultaneously recognizing license plates and timestamps for secure logging.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual feedback, displaying a running count of loaded items and highlighting the specific bag or truck currently being logged to prevent tallying errors and theft.

## Grounding
"Theft during loading" is a major cost for commodity exporters in Pakistan.

## MOAT
License plate recognition and bag-type dataset for local Pakistani logistics.

## Scoring Debate
- **Solution Quality (9)**: Direct anti-corruption and anti-theft tool.
- **Adoption Criteria (8)**: High; commodity traders lose significant money to "missing bags."
- **Moat Confidence (7)**: Moderate; standard computer vision, but the local "License Plate" and "Bag Type" models are niche.
