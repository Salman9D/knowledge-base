# Sports Goods Stitching/Assembly Assistant

**Summary**: AR/Tablet overlay to ensure panel alignment and stitching uniformity in hand-stitched sports goods.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
AR/Tablet overlay for hand-stitched or thermo-molded footballs and cricket bats. Ensures panel alignment and stitching uniformity.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to monitor the assembly of hand-stitched sports goods, ensuring panel symmetry and uniform stitch density.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual guidance, overlaying ideal stitch paths and panel alignment markers directly onto the sports equipment (e.g., football, cricket bat) during the assembly process.

## Grounding
Major global brands (Adidas, Nike) source from Pakistan. Rejections hit margins hard.
