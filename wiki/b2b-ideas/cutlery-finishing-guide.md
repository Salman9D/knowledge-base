# Cutlery Hand-Finishing Guide

**Summary**: Tablet-based visual guide for artisans to ensure uniform blade edge angles and handle polishing.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Wazirabad is the "City of Cutlery." Production is fragmented into small workshops. This tablet guide uses a simple AR overlay to show the artisan the correct grinding angle for specific knife models, ensuring consistency across a "dozen" set.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to measure grinding angles and edge symmetry in real-time during the hand-finishing process.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides a simple AR overlay that projects the ideal grinding angle and shape onto the blade as the artisan works, ensuring uniformity across the entire set.

## Grounding
Local cutlery struggled to scale to high-end global markets because each piece in a set looks slightly different.

Dataset of traditional Wazirabad handle-shaping and blade-grinding workflows.
