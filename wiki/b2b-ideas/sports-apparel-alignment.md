# Sports Apparel Sublimation Alignment

**Summary**: Vision assistant to ensure perfect alignment of digital prints on polyester sports jerseys before heat pressing.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Sublimation printing is standard for Sialkot's export jerseys. Even a 2mm shift in alignment during the heat press stage ruins the garment. This vision system scans the fabric placement and overlays a "digital ghost" of the print to ensure 100% alignment accuracy.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to precisely detect fabric placement and identify even minor misalignments (down to 2mm) before heat pressing.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides a tablet-first visual guide by overlaying a "digital ghost" of the print onto the physical fabric, showing the worker exactly how to adjust the placement for perfect alignment.

## Grounding
Sialkot exports hundreds of millions of dollars in sportswear. Misalignment is the #1 cause of "Seconds" (defective stock) which is then sold locally at a loss.

## MOAT
Dataset of seam and pattern recognition for specific sports silhouettes (FIFA, NBA, NFL standard cuts).

## Scoring Debate
- **Solution Quality (9)**: Solves a high-precision problem that human eyes struggle with under high-volume fatigue.
- **Adoption Criteria (7)**: Requires a fixed camera rig above the heat press, slightly more complex than a handheld tablet, but saves significant fabric waste.
- **Moat Confidence (9)**: Highly specific to the cutting patterns used by Sialkot's export factories.
