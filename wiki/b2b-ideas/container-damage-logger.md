# Port Qasim Container Damage Logger

**Summary**: Automated scanning and logging of structural damage to shipping containers.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Tablet scans shipping containers as they are offloaded, automatically logging structural damage, rust, and seal integrity.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to automatically detect and classify structural damage, rust patches, or seal breaches as containers are moved.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual guidance, highlighting detected defects on the container's surface in real-time and guiding the inspector through a standardized logging workflow.

## Grounding
High volume of imports; dispute resolution over damaged goods is a massive pain point.

## MOAT
Proprietary visual catalog of container logistics wear.
