# Diabetic Foot Ulcer Monitoring Assistant

**Summary**: Tablet-based visual monitoring and progression tracking of diabetic foot ulcers for home-care and clinics.

**Sources**: [[healthcare-xr]], [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Diabetes is an epidemic in Pakistan. Foot ulcers often lead to amputations if not monitored. This tool uses a tablet to scan the ulcer, measuring area, depth (via AR depth sensors), and color changes (indicating infection) to alert doctors of deterioration.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision and depth sensors to accurately measure ulcer area, depth, and color changes indicative of infection.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual guidance, overlaying historical progression data and measurement grids directly over the live view of the wound to help clinicians track healing.

## Grounding
Pakistan has one of the highest diabetes rates in the world (~33M+). Clinical follow-up is poor.

## MOAT
Dataset of diabetic ulcer progression in the local population (skin tones, common infections like MRSA).

## Scoring Debate
- **Solution Quality (9)**: Provides objective clinical data that is currently handled via vague phone photos.
- **Adoption Criteria (7)**: High willingness from private hospitals and home-nursing services.
- **Moat Confidence (9)**: Localized medical datasets are highly regulated and defensible.
