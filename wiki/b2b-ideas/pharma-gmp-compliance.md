# Pharma Lab GMP Compliance Enforcer

**Summary**: Tablet checks for PPE and labeling compliance to ensure GMP standards in pharma labs.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Tablet checks PPE compliance, verifies raw compound labels before mixing, and logs steps for DRAP/FDA audits.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to verify PPE compliance (masks, gloves, gowns) and cross-reference raw compound labels against digital batch records before mixing.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual guidance, overlaying step-by-step compounding instructions and safety warnings directly onto the lab equipment and containers to ensure strict GMP compliance.

## Grounding
Pharmaceutical batch failures or audit failures cost millions (Getz, Searle, Highnoon).

## MOAT
Regulatory audit trail data and specific drug compounding error logs.
