# Tactile Training for Commercial Jet Landing Gear Overhaul

![[problem_24_img1.jpeg]]
![[problem_24_img2.jpeg]]

**Summary**: 1:1 holographic projection and hand tracking for training mechanics on the complex overhaul procedures of commercial jet landing gear.

**Sources**: [[B2B Problem Statements (AR_VR_MR).pdf]]

---

## Description
Overhaul times for commercial jet landing gear are critical; every day an aircraft is grounded represents massive lost revenue. The procedure is extremely complex, requiring high precision and specialized tools. Traditional training involves shadowing senior mechanics, which is slow and inconsistent.
A 1:1 holographic projection allows mechanics to train on a virtual landing gear that mimics the exact physical constraints of the real assembly.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Uses precise hand and finger tracking to assess the trainee's proficiency in manipulating components and using specialized tools. It provides real-time corrective feedback on grip, torque, and sequencing.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Projects a 1:1 scale, high-fidelity holographic model of the landing gear. Trainees can "see through" the assembly to understand internal mechanisms and fluid paths.

## Grounding
The aviation industry faces a global shortage of master mechanics. Accelerated, high-fidelity training reduces the time-to-competency for new technicians and ensures consistent, high-quality overhauls across different maintenance bases.

## MOAT
Capturing the tacit knowledge of master mechanics (the "feel" of a correct fit) into a digital competency progression database that cannot be easily replicated by generic training platforms.
