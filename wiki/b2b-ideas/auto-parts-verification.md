# Auto-Parts Assembly Verification

**Summary**: Step-by-step verification for local auto-part vendors to ensure correct assembly and no missing components.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Tablet-first step-by-step verification for local auto-part vendors supplying Pak Suzuki, Millat Tractors, and Atlas Honda. Ensures no missing bolts or inverted brackets.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to identify each component and verify its placement, ensuring no bolts are missing or brackets are inverted during assembly.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual guidance by highlighting exactly where each part should be installed and projecting the next assembly step directly onto the live feed of the component.

## Grounding
Local auto localization relies on tier-2 vendors with high manual labor dependency.

## MOAT
Assembly failure rates and defect patterns per specific local part.
