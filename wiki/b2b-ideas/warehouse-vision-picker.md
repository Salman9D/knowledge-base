# High-Velocity Picking for E-Commerce Micro-Fulfillment Centers

![[problem_26_img1.jpeg]]
![[problem_26_img2.jpeg]]

**Summary**: Visual routing and item verification for ultra-fast warehouse picking in micro-fulfillment centers to reduce errors and increase throughput.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]], [[B2B Problem Statements (AR_VR_MR).pdf]]

---

## Description
E-commerce delivery speed is increasingly dependent on "micro-fulfillment centers"—small, dense urban warehouses. Picking in these environments requires extreme speed and precision. 
Hands-free (or tablet-mounted cart) routing guides workers to specific bins, while computer vision provides instant verification of picked items, eliminating the need for slow manual barcode scanning.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to verify picked items against the order list and identify the most efficient route through dense shelving without requiring barcode scanning.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides hands-free visual guidance, projecting routing paths and highlighting the correct bin and item to pick directly in the worker's field of view (via smart glasses or AR-enabled tablets).

## Grounding
E-commerce is booming (Daraz, Munchies); logistics requires speed and near-zero error rates. Micro-fulfillment centers are the next frontier for 30-minute delivery promises.

## MOAT
SKU visual data at scale + warehouse layout optimization maps derived from thousands of picking hours.
