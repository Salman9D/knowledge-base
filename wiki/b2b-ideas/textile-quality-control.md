# Textile Quality Control & Defect Detection

**Summary**: Tablet-based real-time scanning of fabric rolls leaving the loom to identify threading defects, color variations, or stains.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]], [[spatial-ai-sync-2026-05-07]]

---

## Description
Tablet-based real-time scanning of fabric rolls leaving the loom. Highlights threading defects, color variations, or stains using local vision models.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to scan fabric rolls at high speed, automatically detecting threading defects, color variations, and stains.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual feedback, highlighting every detected defect in real-time on the live feed of the loom and generating a spatial map of fabric quality for each roll.

## Grounding
Textile is Pakistan's largest export (~60%). Quality rejections by Western buyers cost millions annually.

## MOAT
A proprietary dataset of local fabric defects specific to Pakistani cotton and loom setups.
