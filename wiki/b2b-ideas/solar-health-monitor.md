# Net-Metering Solar Panel Health Monitor

**Summary**: Tablet-based thermal/visual analysis of rooftop solar installations to detect dust-layering or cell hot-spots.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Pakistan has seen a massive surge in rooftop solar. Dust and heat "hot-spots" significantly reduce efficiency. A technician uses a tablet to scan the array; the vision model identifies panels that need cleaning or have micro-cracks.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to identify dust buildup, micro-cracks, and thermal hot-spots on solar panels that lead to performance degradation.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual guidance, highlighting specific panels needing cleaning or repair and overlaying real-time efficiency metrics directly onto the physical solar array.

## Grounding
Pakistan's solar market is booming due to high grid tariffs. Most users don't know why their "10KW" system is only producing 4KW.

## MOAT
Dataset of performance degradation patterns specific to Pakistani dust/pollution profiles.

## Scoring Debate
- **Solution Quality (8)**: Much faster than manual voltage testing of individual panels.
- **Adoption Criteria (8)**: Solar maintenance companies are a rapidly growing B2B sector.
- **Moat Confidence (9)**: Highly localized; Pakistani "calima" dust behaves differently than European dust.
