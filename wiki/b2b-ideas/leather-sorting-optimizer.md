# Leather Hide Sorting & Cutting Optimizer

**Summary**: Tablet-based scanning of raw hides for defects to optimize cutting patterns and maximize yield.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Tablet-mounted over sorting tables. Scans raw hides for scars, tick marks, and color consistency, overlaying the most optimal cutting pattern to maximize yield.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to scan raw hides for scars, tick marks, and color variations that affect leather quality.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual guidance, overlaying the most efficient cutting patterns directly onto the hide to maximize yield and minimize waste around detected defects.

## Grounding
Leather cutting relies heavily on master cutters; waste is high.

## MOAT
Visual data on Pakistani leather defects and optimal algorithmic cutting yields.
