# Termite Infestation Early Detection

**Summary**: Tablet-based visual analysis of wood structures and "mud tubes" to identify termite activity before structural damage.

**Sources**: [[pakistan-b2b-ideas-20]]

---

## Description
Termites are a massive problem in Pakistani wood-heavy construction (roof beams, doors). A technician uses a tablet to scan corners and joints; the vision model identifies early-stage "mud tubes" or wood-surface patterns indicative of infestation.

## Spatial AI Solution
- **AI Spatial Assistant**: Acts as a real-time "expert eye," using computer vision to detect early-stage "mud tubes" and surface patterns indicative of termite activity that are often missed by the human eye.
- **AR/VR Overlay**: Provides tablet-first visual guidance, highlighting identified infestation points and overlaying structural diagrams to show the potential path of damage within the building's framework.

## Grounding
DHA/Bahria Town houses often suffer massive structural damage because termites are caught too late.

## MOAT
A visual catalog of Pakistani termite species' activity markers (e.g., *Heterotermes indicola*).

## Scoring Debate
- **Solution Quality (7)**: Good diagnostic tool, though it doesn't replace professional treatment.
- **Adoption Criteria (7)**: Pest control companies can use this as a "Value-Add" inspection tool to win contracts.
- **Moat Confidence (10)**: Extremely high; very specific local biological markers.
