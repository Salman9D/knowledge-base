## Core Capabilities

Niantic is shifting from an AR game studio (Pokémon GO) into a foundational spatial infrastructure provider.

1. **Large Geospatial Model (LGM)**: Trained on more than **30 billion posed images**, allowing for centimeter-scale global positioning.
2. **Visual Positioning System (VPS)**: Enables devices to understand exactly where they are in the physical world and "pin" persistent digital content to specific locations.
3. **Gaussian Splatting SDK**: Tools for creating high-fidelity 3D reconstructions of real-world spaces.

## Strategic Importance

Niantic is one of the few players attempting to build a **persistent map of the world** that is hardware-agnostic. For 9D Technologies, Niantic represents a potential infrastructure partner for the "Contextual Recall" and "Vertical Navigation" layers of a spatial assistant.
