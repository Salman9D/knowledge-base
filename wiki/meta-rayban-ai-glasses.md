## Product Overview

Meta Ray-Ban AI Glasses represent the leading ambient AI wearable in the market and the "Trojan Horse" of consumer XR:

- **Always-available voice AI** — persistent assistant via Llama 3 models.
- **2025 Volume**: 7M+ units sold in 2025, driving the 44.4% expansion of the smart glasses market.
- **Blayzer + Scriber Optics**: $499 prescription-optimized variant launched Apr 2026 with prescription-ready frames.
- **Neural Band**: Optional accessory for extending processing and battery life.

## Strategic Position

Meta's advantage lies in style-first hardware that users are willing to wear all day. By removing the weight and social friction of a full display, Meta has captured the volume leader position. However, it lacks the persistent spatial memory required for a true Spatial AI Assistant.
