## Market Structure (The Three-Layer Model)

The 2026 landscape is organized into three distinct tiers:
1. **Platform & Device Ecosystems**: Companies owning the OS, hardware, and primary user relationship (Meta, Apple, Google/Samsung).
2. **Enterprise & Workflow Specialists**: ROI-driven firms selling into industrial, healthcare, and simulation niches (PTC, TeamViewer, Varjo, Magic Leap).
3. **Frontier Enablers & Domain Expanders**: Infrastructure and AI firms extending the category (NVIDIA, Qualcomm, Niantic Spatial, World Labs).

## Strategic Summaries

### The Incumbent (Meta)
Meta dominates through scale. By subsidizing Quest hardware and selling the Ray-Ban "Trojan Horse," they own the consumer entry point. However, Reality Labs remains structurally unprofitable despite $2.2B in revenue.

### The Open Stack (Google/Samsung/Qualcomm)
This is the "Android Playbook" for XR. Google provides the OS/AI (Android XR + Gemini), Samsung provides the hardware (Galaxy XR), and Qualcomm provides the silicon. This is the primary challenger to Apple and Meta.

### The Domain Expanders (World Labs/Niantic)
These firms are building the machine-readable world. **Niantic Spatial** is building a Large Geospatial Model, while **World Labs** is building foundational models that understand 3D physics and world state — moving XR from "display" to "intelligence."
