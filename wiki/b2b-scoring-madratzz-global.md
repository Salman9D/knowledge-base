# B2B Idea Scoring - Global

**Summary**: A critical evaluation of B2B Spatial AI ideas through the lens of international market scaling, regulatory complexity (GDPR/HIPAA), and legacy system integration.

**Sources**: [[b2b-eval-criteria-G-raza]], [[market-size-overview]], [[xr-market-signals-2026]]

**Last updated**: 2026-05-15

---

## Evaluation Methodology

Ideas are scored from 1-10 based on three categories, through the lens of international enterprise requirements:
1.  **Quality of Idea**: Strategic value and labor-cost reduction potential.
2.  **Moat**: Defensibility against Big Tech platforms (Meta/Google).
3.  **Adoption (Critical)**: Ease of integration with legacy ERPs and compliance with global privacy laws.

---

## 1. Top Tier (High-Value Global Targets)

### Remote Surgical Mentorship (Global/Rural)
*   **Quality**: 10/10
*   **Moat**: 8/10 (Requires specialized clinical knowledge integration)
*   **Adoption**: 7/10
*   **Critical Critique**: Massive ROI in reducing expert travel. **Downside**: Extremely high liability. A single failure during a mentored surgery could lead to catastrophic lawsuits in Western markets.
*   **Adoption Blocker**: HIPAA/GDPR compliance for streaming live surgical feeds is technically expensive and legally fragile.

### Warehouse Vision Picker (High-Velocity)
*   **Quality**: 9/10
*   **Moat**: 6/10 (Risk of Big Tech like Google/Zebra building this into their OS)
*   **Adoption**: 9/10
*   **Critical Critique**: Proved ROI (PwC/DHL data). **Downside**: High "system fatigue" for workers wearing headsets for 8+ hours.
*   **Adoption Blocker**: None significant, but "Moat" is weak. You are competing with $100B hardware/platform giants directly.

---

## 2. Mid Tier (High Effort, High Reward)

### Pharma Lab GMP Compliance (Global)
*   **Quality**: 9/10
*   **Moat**: 9/10 (Specific regulatory workflow encoding)
*   **Adoption**: 4/10
*   **Critical Critique**: **The "SAP Wall"**. Global pharma companies will not adopt a standalone tool. It *must* integrate with their existing ERP/LIMS.
*   **Adoption Blocker**: The technical debt of building custom connectors for every major global pharma client is a massive scaling bottleneck.

### Industrial Digital Twin (Machine Health)
*   **Quality**: 8/10
*   **Moat**: 7/10
*   **Adoption**: 5/10
*   **Critical Critique**: High saturation. Every major industrial player (Siemens, Bosch, GE) is already building their own XR twins.
*   **Adoption Blocker**: "Not Invented Here" syndrome. Large global enterprises prefer in-house or Tier-1 vendor solutions over startups for core "Digital Twin" infrastructure.

---

## 3. Low Tier (Strategic Mismatch)

### Real Estate Visualization (XR)
*   **Quality**: 6/10
*   **Moat**: 2/10
*   **Adoption**: 8/10
*   **Critical Critique**: **Commoditized**. This is already a saturated market with cheap mobile apps and Matterport-style solutions.
*   **Adoption Blocker**: Low strategic value for a Spatial AI "Assistant" company. This is a visualization tool, not an intelligent assistant.

### Interior Designer Assistant
*   **Quality**: 7/10
*   **Moat**: 3/10 (Apple/Google are building "RoomPlan" and similar APIs directly into the phone)
*   **Adoption**: 9/10
*   **Critical Critique**: **Big Tech Collision**. This is a feature, not a company.
*   **Adoption Blocker**: Within 12-24 months, this will be a "free" feature in iOS/Android XR.
