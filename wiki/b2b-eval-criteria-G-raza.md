# B2B Evaluation Criteria - Global

**Summary**: A brainstorm of international market dynamics, scaling challenges, and technical limitations for AR/VR B2B solutions outside of the local Pakistani context.

**Sources**: [[xr-market-signals-2026]], [[market-size-overview]], User Request

**Last updated**: 2026-05-15

---

## 1. Global Market Dynamics

International markets present a different set of opportunities driven by labor shortages and high operational costs.

### A. Aging Workforce Demographics
*   **The Trend**: Many Western and East Asian economies (USA, Japan, Germany) face an aging population in specialized trades (e.g., senior machinists, specialized surgeons).
*   **Opportunity**: High demand for [[xr-simulations-training]] and "Remote Assist" tools to capture the knowledge of retiring experts and rapidly train new staff.

### B. High Labor Costs & ROI
*   **The Trend**: Rising minimum wages and specialized labor costs make even marginal productivity gains (10-15%) from Spatial AI highly attractive to global enterprises.
*   **Segment**: Logistics, automated warehouses, and high-precision manufacturing.

---

## 2. Scaling & Technical Limitations

While hardware access is easier globally, the "friction to entry" shifts to regulatory and architectural complexity.

### A. Strict Data Privacy & Security
*   **The Issue**: Regulations like **GDPR** (Europe) and **HIPAA** (USA) impose massive penalties for data mishandling, especially with camera-equipped devices (biometric data, face recognition).
*   **Requirement**: Solutions must offer robust end-to-end encryption, on-device processing, and granular privacy controls.

### B. Legacy ERP & IT Integration
*   **The Issue**: Unlike the Pakistani "leapfrog" opportunity, global firms are often locked into complex ecosystems (SAP, Salesforce, Oracle).
*   **Requirement**: Spatial AI tools cannot be "silos"; they must integrate via APIs with existing Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems to track inventory, progress, and performance.

### C. Safety & Health Certifications
*   **The Issue**: Deploying hardware on a shop floor or in a hospital requires passing stringent safety standards (OSHA, FDA, CE marks).
*   **Requirement**: Headsets must be certified as safe for long-term use and must not impede peripheral vision in dangerous environments.

### D. High UX Expectations
*   **The Issue**: Global users have zero tolerance for clunky, unintuitive software. The "Consumerization of IT" means B2B tools must feel as polished as consumer apps.
*   **Requirement**: Investment in high-fidelity 3D assets and seamless user-journey mapping.

---

## 3. Key Growth Segments

*   **Healthcare**: Remote surgery assistance, AR-guided physical therapy, and VR-based medical training.
*   **Advanced Manufacturing**: Digital Twins for real-time monitoring and "just-in-time" AR instruction sets for complex assemblies.
*   **Logistics**: Hands-free picking and navigation in massive, high-volume distribution centers.
