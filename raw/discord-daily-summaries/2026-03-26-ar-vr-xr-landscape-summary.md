# AR/VR/XR Landscape Summary - 2026-03-26

## Summary
Today’s AR/VR/XR signal is more about ecosystem execution than a fresh platform reset. No major same-day first-party Apple, Meta, or Google SDK/platform release surfaced in the tracked source set during live retrieval. The strongest developments were:
- Google continuing public Android/XR demos at MWC Barcelona
- Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024 bringing PS VR2 support in April
- Meta reinforcing long-horizon wearable/XR input work through sEMG research funding
- A dense VR Games Showcase slate showing the software pipeline remains active across Quest, PS VR2, and PC VR

## 1. Major platform updates
- **No major same-day official platform release found** from Apple, Meta, or Google in the tracked source set this morning.
- **Google / Android XR:** Google’s MWC Barcelona 2026 post highlighted demos involving an XR headset and prototype glasses. This is not a launch, but it is a useful signal that Android XR remains active in public-facing demos.
- **Apple / visionOS ecosystem:** The freshest Apple-adjacent item was not a first-party platform update, but a new Vision Pro-only app, AuraTap, built around Persona avatars and launching March 27.

## 2. Notable hardware / product announcements
- **Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024 on PS VR2:** The PS5 version is getting PS VR2 support via the free “Sim 5” update in April. This adds an important high-fidelity sim use case to PS VR2.
- **VR Games Showcase (March 2026):**
  - *PAYDAY: Aces High* announced for Quest and PC VR in 2026
  - *Compass* announced for Quest, PS VR2, and PC VR for Spring 2026
  - *Forefront* 1.0 dated for April 23
  - *Roboquest VR* Quest version + co-op mode dated for May 21
  - *Wrath: Aeon of Ruin VR: Brutal Edition* dated for April 9
- **Google demo signal:** Google’s XR headset and prototype glasses demos remain worth watching as indicators of continuing hardware ambition even without a shipping announcement.

## 3. Developer / tooling / OpenXR
- **PS VR2 optimization details matter:** Microsoft/Asobo emphasized foveated rendering and frame-duplication techniques to make Flight Simulator work on PS VR2. That is a concrete reminder that advanced rendering optimization remains central to premium XR deployment.
- **Meta’s sEMG input work is still alive:** Meta funded six university teams studying learning, onboarding, and expanded use cases for its wrist-worn sEMG interface. This is a meaningful developer signal for future glasses/XR input beyond controllers.
- **No fresh same-day OpenXR standards update found:** In live retrieval, the most relevant official standards context remains OpenXR 1.1, which continues to reduce fragmentation by moving commonly used extensions into core.

## 4. Market / adoption signals
- **Software cadence is the clearest positive signal today.** The VR Games Showcase delivered a substantial batch of release dates and cross-platform content updates.

- **PS VR2 still attracts meaningful support** for premium, technically heavy software when the use case is compelling.
- **Vision Pro remains niche but active.** AuraTap is notable because it targets a narrow but potentially realistic Vision Pro use case: high-end social/networking built around Persona avatars rather than broad consumer social VR.
