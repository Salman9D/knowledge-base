## Actionable takeaway
The XR landscape is rapidly shifting from VR-centric to AR/mixed reality with phone integration as the near-term priority. Developers should focus on utility-driven AR experiences (navigation, contextual info, hands-free flows) rather than immersive VR, while monitoring privacy developments around facial recognition in smart glasses. Meta's Horizon shift to mobile and Google's Android XR push signal the industry believes scale comes through smartphones, not standalone headsets.
