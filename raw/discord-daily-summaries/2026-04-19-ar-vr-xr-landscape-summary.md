# AR/VR/XR Landscape Summary - 2026-04-19

## Major platform updates
- Meta Quest 3 and Quest 3S price increase effective today (April 19, 2026): Quest 3S 128GB $300→$350, 256GB $400→$450; Quest 3 512GB $500→$600 due to memory chip cost surge.
- Meta upgraded to Promoter membership in the Khronos Group, gaining a seat on the Board of Directors.
- Android XR now offers autospatialization of 2D apps; over 100 MR/VR apps built for Android XR; 45% increase in spatialized 2D applications signaling a shift toward spatial productivity.

## Notable hardware / product announcements
- Apple-EssilorLuxottica Gen-3 Vision Pro microLED waveguides (reported April 10) reducing 3D camera costs by 40%, though not a direct consumer launch.
- No other major hardware announcements today.

## Developer / tooling / OpenXR
- Khronos OpenXR Extension Matrix updated with middleware support data from Godot, LÖVR, StereoKit, Unity, and Unreal Engine.
- Khronos released new OpenVX extensions (Target Kernel, Node Command) to streamline compute workloads on heterogeneous SoCs.

## Market / adoption signals
- Global Extended Reality (XR) Market 2026-2036 report released April 16, projecting market size to reach USD 145.03 Bn by 2032.
- Apple’s Vision Pro (launched Feb 2024) remains the highest average selling price consumer spatial computing device.
- Android XR adoption growing, with developers moving from VR gaming to spatial productivity apps.
