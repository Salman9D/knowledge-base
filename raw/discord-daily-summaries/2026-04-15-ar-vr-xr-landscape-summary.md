# AR/VR/XR Landscape Summary - 2026-04-15

## Major platform updates
- Apple’s Business app (v2.0) now supports Apple Vision Pro for the first time, adding directory features and a refreshed UI for enterprise users.

## Notable hardware / product announcements
- Operator XR launched **Interceptor**, a portable VR counter‑drone training platform for military and law enforcement, deployable in under two minutes.
- Snap confirmed a multi‑year chip deal with Qualcomm to supply Snapdragon XR platforms for its upcoming consumer AR glasses (Specs line).
- Blackmagic’s URSA Cine Immersive camera for Apple Vision Pro content is now orderable at ~$30K, enabling end‑to‑end immersive video workflow.
- Leaks indicate Apple is testing four smart‑glass frame designs (two rectangular, two oval) in 2026, with a possible late‑2026 unveil and 2027 retail.

## Developer / tooling / OpenXR
- SteamVR Beta released a fix for an OpenXR crash related to `xrDestroyInstance`, improving stability for Linux VR users.

## Market / adoption signals
- Maximize Market Research projects the global AR/VR market to grow from $41.5B in 2025 to $145.0B by 2032 (CAGR 19.6%), driven by enterprise and industrial adoption.
