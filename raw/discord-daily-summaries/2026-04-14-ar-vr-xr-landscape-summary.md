# AR/VR/XR Landscape Summary - 2026-04-14

## Major Platform Updates

- **Apple visionOS 26.5 developer betas** released April 13, 2026 (second beta), following visionOS 26.4 public release (March 24) with 8 new emoji, bug fixes, and security updates
- **Meta Quest v2.3** release notes from week of March 23, 2026 (latest public update)

## Notable Hardware/Product Announcements
- **Snap-Qualcomm partnership** (April 10, 2026): Multi-year agreement to power Specs Inc.'s consumer AR glasses with Snapdragon XR platforms, enabling on-device AI, cutting-edge graphics, and advanced multiuser experiences
- **Samsung Galaxy XR**: Receiving 3 major upgrades (reported Dec 2025) including camera passthrough improvements, positioning it as a Vision Pro alternative at ~$1,799
- **Valve Steam Frame**: Announced Nov 2025 for early 2026 release, marking Valve's re-entry into VR hardware
- **Xreal 1S**: $449, 85g AR glasses; **Viture Beast**: $550 with enhanced contrast for creators

## Developer/Tooling/OpenXR
- **Google Vibe Coding XR**: Preparing to showcase at ACM CHI 2026 (Apr 13-17) for rapid VR app development via Gemini blocks
- **OpenXR advancements**: Eye-tracked Variable Rate Shading (VRS) support for foveated rendering; Godot integration project features now production-ready
- **Android XR ecosystem**: Expanding via Google's Project Aura with Asus ROG partnerships and Samsung flat-AR display glasses

## Market/Adoption Signals
- **IDC projection**: Vision Pro and competitors could drive 40M+ XR hardware units/year by 2026

- **Samsung Galaxy XR**: Analysts project >100k unit sales in 2026 for this Android XR headset
