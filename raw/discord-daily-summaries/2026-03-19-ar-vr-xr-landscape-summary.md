# AR/VR/XR Landscape Summary - 2026-03-19

## Major platform updates
- **Meta Quest ecosystem:** Horizon Worlds disappears from the Quest store on Mar 31 and loses VR access entirely on Jun 15, while Hyperscape sharing ends Mar 24 and Horizon+ avatar perks sunset Mar 31. Meta says the pivot frees it to invest in Quest OS productivity upgrades (Surface Keyboard, Touchpad, Navigator UI).  
  Source: https://communityforums.atmeta.com/blog/AnnouncementsBlog/updates-to-your-meta-quest-experience-in-2026/1369435
- **Pico OS 6:** Spatial Engine lets standard Android apps live inside MR layouts with 360° PanoScreen multitasking and mixed input (hands, controllers, KB/M), signaling ByteDance’s intent to make Project Swan a full spatial-computing platform.  
  Source: https://www.roadtovr.com/pico-project-swan-os-6-update/

## Notable hardware / product announcements
- **Valve Steam Frame:** All three new Valve devices (Steam Frame, Steam Machine, Steam Controller) now show “coming soon” in Steam’s backend, and Valve still targets 1H26 shipping despite RAM/storage shortages—expect sudden retail availability once parts stabilize.  
  Source: https://www.roadtovr.com/valve-steam-frame-coming-soon-2026/
- **Pico Project Swan:** Late-2026 headset promises MicroOLED displays near 4,000 ppi, >45 PPD center clarity, and a dual-chip design (custom perception silicon + >2× XR2 Gen 2 SoC), with a global early-access tester program launching via picoxr.com.  
  Source: https://www.roadtovr.com/pico-project-swan-os-6-update/

## Developer / tooling / OpenXR
- **Apple visionOS 26.4:** Foundation Models updates change instruction-following and tool-calling, and Apple posted new Python bindings so teams can regression-test prompts locally before customers upgrade.

Source: https://developer.apple.com/hello/march26/
- **Godot XR toolchain:** Godot 4.6 now initializes OpenXR 1.1 automatically, adds a Project Setup Wizard, strengthens Vulkan multithreading, and embeds the Khronos loader into Android builds, easing multi-headset deployment.  
  Source: https://godotengine.org/article/godot-xr-update-mar-2026/

## Market / adoption signals
- **VITURE’s $100 M round:** Legend Capital led another nine-figure raise (now $200 M total in six months) to scale Luma/Beast glasses and counter XREAL’s litigation, highlighting investor appetite for tethered XR eyewear ahead of Android XR’s launch window.  
  Source: https://www.roadtovr.com/xr-glasse-viture-100m-financing-2026/
- **Horizon+ scale:** Meta disclosed 1 M+ active Horizon+ subscribers and YoY Quest in-app payment growth of 13%, anchoring a $60–96 M subscription run-rate even without new 2025 hardware.  
  Source: https://www.roadtovr.com/metas-horizon-plus-1m-active-subscriber/
