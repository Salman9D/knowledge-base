# AR/VR/XR Landscape Summary - 2026-04-20

## Major platform updates
- Apple releases visionOS 26.4 (April 16) and visionOS 26.5 beta (April 13).
- Apple Business app now supports Vision Pro (April 14).
- Steam Link coming to Vision Pro (April 13).
- Samsung Galaxy XR launches with Android XR OS (today).

## Notable hardware/product announcements
- Samsung Galaxy XR headset: $1,799, 4K Micro-OLED, Snapdragon XR2+ Gen 2, Gemini AI, external battery (2.5h video), 545g.
- Meta Quest 3 price increased by $100 effective April 19 (now $599 for 128GB model) due to global RAM crisis.

## Developer/tooling/OpenXR
- Khronos adds VDXR runtime (OpenXR alternative to SteamVR) (April 18).
- Ron Bessems (Meta) appointed Director at Khronos Board (April 18).
- OpenXR CTS improvements underway (contractor task for conformance tests) (April 16).
- Android XR Spatial Computing guide highlights Auto-Spatialization: toggle 2D YouTube to 3D Depth mode (April 17).
- UploadVR: Quest Capture Markers feature improves screenshots/video framing (April 19).

## Market/adoption signals
- Samsung Galaxy XR launch intensifies mixed-reality competition with Apple Vision Pro.
- Meta Quest price hike reflects component cost pressures; may slow VR adoption.
- Apple Business app support signals growing enterprise Vision Pro use.
- Steam Link on Vision Pro expands gaming/content accessibility.
