# AR/VR/XR Landscape Summary - 2026-04-17

## Major platform updates
- Meta Platforms announced price increases for Quest VR headsets effective April 19, 2026 due to rising memory chip costs: Quest 3S up $50, Quest 3 up $100 (Reuters, Bloomberg).
- Android XR platform progress: Google announced partnerships with frame makers (Gucci) and Samsung for 2026 launches; Samsung Galaxy XR received a recent security patch and new features (Android Central, 9to5Google).
- Apple reportedly testing four smart‑glass designs and developing its own AI visual models for future glasses (PhoneArena, Glass Almanac, MacRumors citing Bloomberg).
- OpenXR Working Group issued an RFP for conformance test suite improvements, focusing on performance settings, debug utils, and composition layer extensions (Khronos).

## Notable hardware / product announcements
- Snap’s consumer Specs AR glasses confirmed to run on Qualcomm Snapdragon XR platform, targeting lightweight everyday use with a 2026 launch (Glass Almanac).
- Gucci‑branded Android XR smart glasses slated for 2027 (9to5Google, mobilesyrup).
- No major new headset launches this week; updates are mainly software/firmware patches.

## Developer / tooling / OpenXR
- OpenXR CTS improvements RFP seeks contractors to write conformance tests and implement test cases for extensions like XR_EXT_performance_settings and XR_EXT_debug_utils, plus support for various composition layers (Khronos RFP).
- No major SDK releases observed this week.

## Market / adoption signals
- Global XR market forecast (2026‑2036) projects steady growth across VR, AR, MR, driven by enterprise and consumer adoption (Globenewswire).
- Entertainment AR/VR System‑on‑Chip market expected to reach $3.81 billion in 2026, led by Apple, Samsung, and Meta (Globenewswire).
- Meta’s price hike reflects component‑cost pressures that could affect near‑term adoption.
