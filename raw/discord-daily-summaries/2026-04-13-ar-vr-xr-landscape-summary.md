# AR/VR/XR Landscape Summary - April 13, 2026

## Major platform updates
- Android XR update launched for Samsung Galaxy XR with auto 2D to 3D conversion, pinned wall apps, improved hand/eye tracking, session resume, and Android Enterprise support for real-world use.
- Apple visionOS 26.4 released for Vision Pro bringing better network performance, 4K 2D streaming, panoramic view adjustments, and a beta Steam Link app for gaming. Challenges in retail demos noted.
- Meta Quest v81 Public Test Channel rollout begins for Quest 2, Pro, 3, and 3S, introducing new UI and features under test.

## Notable hardware / product announcements
- No new hardware launches today.
- Ongoing anticipation for Snap Specs AR glasses powered by Snapdragon XR chipset set for late 2026.
- Samsung Galaxy XR's Android Enterprise update improves viability for healthcare and retail sectors.

## Developer / tooling / OpenXR
- Khronos extends OpenXR Extension Matrix with middleware implementation data from top game engines (Godot, Unity, Unreal, etc.) improving developer transparency on XR stack capabilities.
- Android XR platform updates simplify building immersive 3D and enterprise XR apps.

## Market / adoption signals

- Major partnerships: Google-Samsung (Android XR) and Snap-Qualcomm (Snapdragon XR) drive ecosystem growth and hardware stability.
- Apple Vision Pro retail launch challenges show importance of demos and trained staff for adoption aside from tech.
- Industry events and hackathons in 2026 show sustained developer and market interest.
