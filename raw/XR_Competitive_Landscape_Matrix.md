# XR Competitive Landscape Matrix

## How to read this

This matrix is meant to be fast to scan inside Obsidian. It is not a market-share table. It is a strategy table.

Scoring key:
- **High** = strong current strategic position
- **Medium** = competitive but not clearly leading
- **Low** = weak or limited position in that dimension
- **N/A** = not a primary focus for that player

## Recommended framing for research use

### Market structure in one line
- **Meta** = incumbent
- **Apple** = premium integrator
- **Google** = ecosystem challenger
- **Qualcomm** = infrastructure enabler
- **HTC/Pico** = alternative hardware bets
- **Unity/Unreal** = developer choke points
- **OpenAI/DeepMind/FAIR** = future intelligence layer contenders

### What matters most over the next 24 months
1. Whether **Android XR** gets real device volume and developer adoption.
2. Whether **Apple** expands beyond ultra-premium pricing.
3. Whether **Meta** can keep its lead while broadening use cases beyond games and fitness.
4. Whether **AI assistants** in XR become truly useful rather than just demo-friendly.
5. Whether **glasses** begin to matter strategically as much as headsets.
